About Me

Research Fellow at Institute of Rural Management Anand (IRMA) with interdisciplinary expertise in Remote Sensing, GIS, Geography, and GeoAI applications for environmental systems.

Lakshmikant Tiwari, M.Tech (Remote Sensing & GIS)

I am passionate about exploring the impact and efficiency of environmental planning and management strategies, drivers, processes and dynamics in changing climate settings for effective environmental monitoring.

My work integrates Geospatial Analytics, satellite data processing, machine learning, and causal inference while mapping urban landscape systems indicators using satellite-derived biophysical parameters.

I have led geospatial analytics for the World Bank-supported REWARD intervention, focusing on hydrological processes modelling for watershed management, disaster resilience, and hydroclimatic dynamics.
